About

Sundar Pichai is an Indian-American business executive and the CEO of Google LLC, one of the world's largest technology companies. Born in Chennai, India, Pichai studied engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur before moving to the United States to attend Stanford University and the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania. Pichai joined Google in 2004 and quickly rose through the ranks, working on products such as Google Chrome and Google Drive. In 2015, he became CEO of Google and its parent company, Alphabet Inc. Pichai is known for his leadership skills, his passion for innovation, and his commitment to creating products that make a positive impact on people's lives.
